About me

For over years I have been working as a comminication designer. Driven by the fact that future design solutions will be technologically dependant, I've focused my work mostly on creative coding, physical computing and digital design in general. Therefore I am constantly expanding my knowledge about the newest technological breakthroughs and thinking about methods of implementing them into new smart creative solutions. I am firmly convinced that interdisciplinary teams are the key to an efficient and innovative output.
